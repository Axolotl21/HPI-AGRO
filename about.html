About Us

History of HPI-Agro

PT HPI-AGRO was founded in 2008 as a national private agribusiness company that focuses on oil palm and other crops plantations. Since its establishment, HPI-Agro has upheld its commitment to building a plantation business that is law-abiding, environmentally friendly and provides added value and benefits to the communities surrounding the plantations. These three aspects are important in order to establish a healthy partnership between the company and the community. In 2010, HPI-Agro started its first planting in one of its oil palm plantations in West Kalimantan. The first planting was carried out simultaneously with the inauguration of the HPI-Agro training center for garden assistants.
