IT Cyber & Risk Analyst

Description

  1. Coordinate, implement, and supervise end-to-end cyber risk assessment, digitalization risk assessment, IT security risk assessment activities.
  2. Analyze and advise mitigation plan for IT and cyber risk.
  3. Implement Astra ERM quarterly top risk reporting, for both IT and non-IT matters (general).
  4. Prepare and present audit report to management.

Requirement

  1. Bachelor or Master Degree from any major, but Information & Technology major is highly preferable.
  2. Minimum 3 years experiences in related field.
  3. Proficient in English both oral & written.
