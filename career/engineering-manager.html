Engineering Manager

Description

  1. Partnering with engineering, product, and design leaders to shape team direction and strategy.
  2. Supporting and driving this strategy to clear impact with the help of your team.
  3. Enabling your team to achieve high levels of productivity, performance and reliability.
  4. Teaching, mentoring, coaching and being a role model for engineers and other product managers.
  5. Creating a collaborative, inclusive and supportive team culture focused on fast, focused, high impact execution.
  6. Helping to shape and evolve our engineering culture by contributing to our engineering organization programs.
  7. Ad-hoc coding. To be an effective manager we believe you need a high degree of empathy and respect. To help with this you'll be getting into the code, perhaps contributing to the team's project, or working on some automation scripts
  8. Optimizing engineering performance.

Requirement

  1. An ability to lead software engineering teams that deliver impact quickly.
  2. Experience managing product software engineers.
  3. Good technical background in software engineering. You have first-hand experience developing software
  4. Experience developing and supporting a team of engineers in their personal growth and career progression.
  5. A technical understanding of web and mobile platforms, the unique opportunities and challenges that come with shipping and maintaining web and mobile products.
  6. Experience in shipping/delivering web and mobile app.
  7. Passionate about recruiting, hiring and growing our software engineering team.
  8. A track record of partnering with product and design leadership to deliver great outcomes.
  9. Experience with different product development methodologies
