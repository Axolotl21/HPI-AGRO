Two HPI Agro Companies Win Certificates of Appreciation from the Governor of West Kalimantan.

A total of 34 oil palm plantation companies in West Kalimantan received certificates of appreciation from the Provincial Government of West Kalimantan. One of them is HPI agro namely PT. Saban Sawit Subur (SSS) and PT. Lingkar Indah Plantation (LIP). This award certificate was handed over to representatives of the HPI agro company on the 64th Birthday (HUT) of the West Kalimantan Provincial Government, Thursday (28/1/2021).

The award certificate was given for the participation and support of palm oil companies to the Provincial Government of West Kalimantan in maintaining provincial road infrastructure in West Kalimantan. Among other things, the award was given to an oil palm plantation company that contributed to developing West Kalimantan.

In his remarks, the Governor of West Kalimantan, Sutarmidji expressed his gratitude to oil palm plantation companies that have contributed to infrastructure development in West Kalimantan.

One of them is in the construction and repair of roads which are the basic infrastructure needed by the community. Thank you to the company that has helped repair the road, even though the asphalt has not been touched but it can still function as it should," said Sutarmidji in his remarks.

Those doing business in West Kalimantan must use Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds for the benefit and acceleration of community welfare," he said.

Separately, the Estate Manager of PT. Lingkar Indah Plantation HPI agro, Erdi said the company has a positive commitment in building road repair infrastructure which is basic infrastructure so that it has a direct impact not only on the company's employees but also the communities around the plantations who feel it.

The smooth public transportation needed by the community through the company's CSR will have an impact on the economy of the community around the plantation. Apart from that, added Erdi, the company also has a commitment to build positive partnerships in the community through the company's CSR funds, explained Erdi to this media.
