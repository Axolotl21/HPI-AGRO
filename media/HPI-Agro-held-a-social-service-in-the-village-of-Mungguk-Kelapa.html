26 September 2022

HPI Agro held a social service in the village of Mungguk Kelapa

This social service activity which was carried out on Thursday, 22 September 2022 is a form of the Company's CSR program with the aim of maintaining good relations with the community around the company.

Also attending the social service activities included the leadership and staff as well as employees of PT BHA 2 to carry out social service activities such as cleaning and repairing the football field, village office and the Mungguk Kelapa Village Hall, in addition to PT BHA's social services with traditional management villages. local cultural customs with pencak silat performances, guest welcoming rituals as a form of pride for the village community because the company has maintained good relations with adat and the community in general.

In his remarks, the Head of Mungguk Kelapa Village expressed his pride and great hopes for PT BHA 2 so that good relations would continue for the common good and progress. "As the Head of Mungguk Kelapa Village, I express my deepest gratitude to PT BHA for participating in helping repair the soccer field, because we plan to hold a soccer tournament to welcome Christmas in 2022," said the man who is familiarly called Jawas.

"We also hope that good relations will continue to be established between the company and the community so that we can progress and prosper together, and we also urge the public to continue to maintain good cooperation with employees and company leaders and not to do things that are detrimental. breaking the law," Jawas added.

The leadership of BHA 2 which was attended directly by Mr. Martono Susilo also felt proud of the community's welcome and hoped that good relations and mutually reinforcing cooperation would be established between the company and the community, "We really appreciate the welcome and banquet from the village government and the indigenous people of Mungguk Kelapa village, we hope this activity can be a good start for establishing mutually reinforcing relationships and also providing benefits for the welfare and progress of villages around the company, especially Mungguk Kelapa village and we hope that employees from the community around the company can further improve their discipline and productivity"
