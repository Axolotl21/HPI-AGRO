HPI Agro held a ceremony for the 77th Indonesian Independence Day at the Factory location

All company employees held a flag ceremony in commemoration of the 77th Anniversary of the Independence of the Republic of Indonesia at the Ruai Mill Palm Oil Factory Location, Tanjung Balai Village, Kuala Behe District Landak Regency Wednesday (17/8/2022)

The ceremony went smoothly followed by the ceremony participants with great solemnity to commemorate the services of the heroes who had fought for the raising of the Red and White flag in Indonesia.

Acting as the inspector at this ceremony was the Group Manager of HPI Agro Area 1 B, Kolbert Ferdinan Simamora.

This ceremony feels very special because as we know, several years ago our country was hit by the Covid 19 pandemic and now is the time for us to rise up to fix everything that is our responsibility as employees to advance the company and be responsible to stakeholders, both government and society, such as the theme national, Recover Faster, Rise Stronger," explained Kolbert.

In his remarks, Kolbert advised the company to come to invest with love and affection, but so far it cannot be denied that there are still deficiencies, so it is time for us to work hand in hand with stakeholders to work hand in hand. build better cooperation so that what is the goal of advancing general welfare and educating the life of the nation can be realized.

"Do not forget that I also appeal to all employees to be able to position themselves as pioneers inviting local residents to jointly maintain security and not hurt each other in order to create a harmonious life and cooperation so that all parties can feel the presence of investment. impact on shared progress," Colbert said.

Meanwhile, the Head of the Administration Section (KTU) of PT Kapuas Rimba Sejahtera, Sohimun said the ceremony was carried out with very minimal preparation but was able to run successfully and smoothly. In addition to carrying out the flag raising, the event continued with cutting the tumpeng as an expression of gratitude on Independence Day.

"We do not forget to express our deepest gratitude to all parties who have participated in making the ceremony for the 77th Indonesian Independence Day," added Sohimun.
